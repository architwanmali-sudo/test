Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

What should I pack for a Himalayan trek?

We provide a detailed packing list after booking. Essentials include layered clothing, sturdy trekking shoes, sunscreen, sunglasses, a small daypack, personal medications, and a water bottle. We provide camping equipment, tents, and sleeping bags.

Are beginners welcome on your treks?

Absolutely! We offer treks for all experience levels. Our easy-to-moderate treks like Beas Kund and Bhrigu Lake are perfect for beginners. Our experienced guides ensure everyone feels safe and supported throughout the journey.

Is camping equipment provided?

Yes! All camping equipment is included — premium quality tents, sleeping bags, sleeping mats, and kitchen equipment. We maintain our gear to the highest standards to ensure your comfort at altitude.

Are your guides certified?

Yes, all our guides are experienced, trained in wilderness first aid, and deeply knowledgeable about Himalayan terrain and culture. Our lead guide, Mangal Thakur, has over 26 years of experience navigating these mountains.

How do I book a trek?

Booking is simple! You can message us on WhatsApp, call us directly at +91 94185 03080, or fill out the contact form on this page. We'll share all available dates, itinerary details, and pricing. A small advance confirms your spot.
